COMMUNITY EXTENSION PROGRAM

The creation of the Community Extension Program is a direct result of the institution’s unwavering dedication to engaging in community education and service. This initiative aligns seamlessly with the overarching vision and mission of the institution, emphasizing the holistic development of students to excel not only academically but also morally and socially.

Functioning holistically, this program acts as the vital bridge connecting the institution with its designated communities, facilitating the exchange of resources and collaboration to promote community empowerment and self-sufficiency.
