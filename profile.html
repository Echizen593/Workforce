Profile

Workforce Career Institute is a non-stock, non-profit educational institution focused on preparing individuals for careers in various industries. It offers a variety of programs aimed at developing specific skills and knowledge relevant to today’s workplace requirements. It further guarantees holistic services and support to its students from admission to graduation.

Core Offerings

  • ✔ Career Mapping Sessions
  • ✔ Dynamic and Collaborative Classroom Environment
  • ✔ Mentoring and Coaching Sessions
  • ✔ Career Support Programs

Workforce Career Institute is the Founder’s dream come true. It all started with an ardent desire to put up an educational institution that would cater to the needs of Filipinos—from career programs to graduate programs.

The pandemic changed the education landscape in the country. The situation inspired the Founder, Dr. Peter Elmar Cahati-an Bordo, to establish an institution that serves as a platform for the continuous delivery of pandemic-proof programs.

Today, Workforce Career Institute fulfills its mission by offering various VocTech, Bachelor’s, Master’s, and Doctoral programs—giving hope to those eager to learn and imbibe the spirit of careerpreneurship.
