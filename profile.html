PROFILE

Workforce Career Institute is a non-stock, non-profit educational institution focused on preparing individuals for careers in various industries...

  • Career Mapping Sessions
  • Dynamic and Collaborative Classroom Environment
  • Mentoring and Coaching Sessions
  • Career Support Programs

Workforce Career Institute is the Founder’s dream come true...

The pandemic changed the education landscape in the country...

Now, Workforce Career Institute is serving its purpose through the delivery of various VocTech, Bachelor’s, Master’s, and Doctoral programs...

VISION

It envisions itself to be the premier career-focused institution in Aklan by helping individuals build and refine their skillsets to become job-ready.

MISSION

  • Develop and implement industry-based programs to meet changing needs.
  • Provide practical education bridging theory and real-world application.
  • Build partnerships with industry players to support student job searches.

CORE VALUES

  • Advocate of distance education and computer-aided learning
  • Believer of lifelong learning
  • Committed to provide quality teaching
  • Dedicated in uplifting teamwork spirit
  • Excellence in post-study support
