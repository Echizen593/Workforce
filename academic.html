Consortium Partnership

Workforce Career Institute has inked a continuous Consortium Partnership with Southern Philippine Academy (SPA) College. Under the partnership, Workforce serves as SPA’s satellite campus in the Visayan Region. Hence, the offering and delivery of SPA’s Bachelor’s, Master’s, and Doctoral programs.
