CAREER CENTER

The primary objective of the Career Center is to assist the institution in fostering a vision among students regarding their future aspirations post-graduation, while concurrently ensuring a well-rounded perspective by enhancing awareness and comprehension of potential career paths associated with their chosen degree programs.

Fundamentally, it is dedicated to delivering comprehensive support for career readiness and furnishing students with the essential skills and competencies required to embark on their professional journeys, whether as members of the workforce or as aspiring entrepreneurs.

Additionally, the scope of its services encompasses aiding graduating students and alumni in identifying opportunities aligned with their qualifications.

Research Opportunities for Transformative Learning

A pivotal element of the rigorous educational framework provided to students at Workforce Career Institute encompasses the acquisition of competencies in conducting high-quality research that fosters redemptive comprehension and pragmatic resolutions to both Philippine and global challenges.

In support of this pedagogical aim, Workforce Career Institute, in cooperation with The Mentor: A Multidisciplinary E-Gazette, facilitates research opportunities for students. Such opportunities may be particularly recommended for students exhibiting exceptional potential for enhanced academic development, as well as for the exploration of prospective life trajectories.
